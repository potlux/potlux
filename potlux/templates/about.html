Mission
To accelerate global environmental progress by unifying the collegiate sustainability movement
We see Universities as incredible petri dishes of environmental innovation. However, the communication mediums between them could be vastly improved. Many college projects and best practices live in isolation or sluggish diffusion.

Potlux looks to address this by being the first online community where collegiate sustainability initiatives are effectively aggregated and shared.

We think the potential for this platform is huge: facilitating best practice movements across the college network, inspiring new ideas, building intercollegiate collaboration, and catalyzing project funding.


Purpose
Collaboration. Knowledge. Innovation.
Universities are at the forefront of environmental innovation; however, the communication mediums between them could be vastly improved. Potlux is a catalogue of ideas and a community of collegiate sustainability leaders. By sharing information and connecting with each other, Universities can adopt the best ideas into their sustainability platform and contact each other for support on how to accomplish this.


Our Name
Potlux: Sharing Light
We love potlucks. Everyone brings what they can to a delicious, community meal. We love the variety, creativity, and sharing culture. Our organization looks to do exactly this, but rather than bringing snacks, everyone contributes their idea, their creativity, their light that collectively grows brighter as more contribute. This concept is embodied in the suffix of our name as "lux" is the SI unit of illuminance.
